Privacy & Consent

Sherpaclinicalstudies.com is a secure, password-protected database. All the information that you choose to share will be kept private and confidential. Read Sherpa Clinical's Notice of Privacy Practices

Sherpa Clinical is a secure tool that facilitates connections between health researchers and patients. Your membership lets us know that you may be interested in participating in clinical studies with our partner organizations, but you are never obligated. You are always free to say no and can specify how often you would like to be contacted. You can deactivate your account at any time. Read Sherpa Clinical Consent Document
